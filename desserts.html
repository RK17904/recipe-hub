Dessert Recipes

Chocolate Cake

Ingredients:

  • Flour
  • Cocoa powder
  • Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Butter

Steps:

  1. Mix dry and wet ingredients separately.
  2. Combine and bake for 30 minutes.
  3. Let cool and serve with frosting.

Fruit Salad

Ingredients:

  • Apples
  • Bananas
  • Grapes
  • Honey

Steps:

  1. Chop all fruits into small pieces.
  2. Mix in a bowl and drizzle with honey.
  3. Chill before serving.
