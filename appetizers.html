Appetizer Recipes

Bruschetta

Ingredients:

  • Baguette slices
  • Tomatoes
  • Basil
  • Garlic

Steps:

  1. Toast the bread slices.
  2. Mix tomatoes, basil, and garlic.
  3. Top bread with the mixture and serve.

Garlic Bread

Ingredients:

  • Bread loaf
  • Butter
  • Garlic
  • Parsley

Steps:

  1. Mix butter, garlic, and parsley.
  2. Spread on bread slices.
  3. Bake until golden brown.
